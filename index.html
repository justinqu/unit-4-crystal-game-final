Welcome to the Crystal Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There will be four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

Select a crystal to make magic happen

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number and you lose by going over the random number.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.

Good Luck!
